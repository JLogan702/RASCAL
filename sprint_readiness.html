How Sprint Readiness Is Calculated

Sprint Readiness represents the percentage of story tickets in future sprints that are already in a “To Do” or “Ready for Development” status. This ensures that each team has planned work that is groomed and actionable before the sprint begins.

Statuses like “New”, “Backlog”, or “Grooming” are not counted toward readiness. However, all statuses are shown for transparency.
