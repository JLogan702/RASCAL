Dependency Tracker

This page helps identify critical cross-team or external blockers.

Dependency tickets are pulled directly from Jira's "Blocks" link fields. These reflect unresolved inbound or outbound blockers. Story tickets with one or more blocking links are listed below. We include ticket summaries to help assess risk and understand impact.

Proactively resolving these items can unblock value delivery and prevent sprint spillover.
