Dependencies

This section will eventually track cross-team dependencies such as "blocks" or "is blocked by" relationships across Jira tickets.

To populate this view, ensure your data includes columns such as 'Inward issue link (Blocks)' and 'Outward issue link (Blocks)'.

If dependencies are provided in a structured format, we will visualize them here with a matrix or timeline chart.
