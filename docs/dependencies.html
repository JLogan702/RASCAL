Dependency Tracker

Overview

This section highlights story-level dependencies between teams. We look for open “blocks” relationships in Jira and break them down by source and target team. Addressing these early is crucial for planning and delivery across sprints.

Dependencies reflect Jira story tickets with 'blocks' issue links. Source: Agile Dependency Management Practices – Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe), Scrum.org, and Atlassian
