Backlog Health Dashboard

Overview

This report shows how much of each team’s backlog is in “groomed” statuses vs how much is unready. We’re using industry best practices to prioritize backlog items that are in New and Grooming for future sprints. A healthy backlog ensures better sprint planning, reduced churn, and faster velocity.

Metrics derived from Jira report — Story tickets only, non-Done, not in active sprints. Source: Agile Alliance, Scrum.org Backlog Best Practices
