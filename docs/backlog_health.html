Backlog Health

Definition: Backlog Health is the percentage of story tickets in statuses “New”, “Grooming”, or “Backlog” that are not in active sprints.

Reference: Scrum.org, Atlassian

=======

Backlog Health Dashboard

This report evaluates backlog health based on the percentage of story tickets in "New," "Grooming," or "Backlog" statuses that are assigned to a future (non-active) sprint or unassigned entirely. This aligns with agile readiness best practices that encourage forward-looking planning and grooming.

Metrics based on industry standards from Scrum.org and Agile Alliance
