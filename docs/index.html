Program Summary Dashboard

These dashboards are designed to inform—not punish. Our goal is to provide objective visibility into the state of story ticket flow across the program. This summary aggregates the status of upcoming sprints and backlog grooming across all teams. Use this as a conversation starter and improvement guide.

The Overall Program Health shown below reflects Sprint Readiness and Backlog Health calculations, following guidance from the Scrum Guide, Agile Manifesto, and industry reports like State of Agile (VersionOne, 2023). Specifically:

  • Sprint Readiness = % of story tickets in future sprints marked as “To Do” or “Ready for Development”
  • Backlog Health = % of all non-done stories in grooming statuses assigned to future sprints
  • Each team has separate calculations displayed on their dedicated tabs

Overall Program Status

This light reflects an aggregate readiness + backlog health score using weighted logic across all five teams. It's designed to help leadership quickly assess if upcoming work is ready, at risk, or blocked from the start.

See Sprint Readiness and Backlog Health pages for individual team metrics, issue counts by status, and explanation of values.

Based on Agile best practices and program standards. Source: Scrum.org, State of Agile Report, Agile Manifesto.
