Program Summary

This report blends Sprint Readiness and Backlog Health to provide a clear overview of each team’s agile preparedness. Metrics are calculated using only future-sprint or backlog-assigned story tickets (excluding Done, Blocked, or In Progress). Readiness includes tickets in “To Do” and “Ready for Development”. Backlog Health includes “New” and “Grooming”.

Standards Used:
• Scrum.org – Sprint Backlog
• Agile Alliance – Backlog Management
• Martin Fowler – Backlog Evolution

These dashboards are designed to help teams reflect and adjust — not to penalize. Data is real, calculated consistently, and updated regularly.
