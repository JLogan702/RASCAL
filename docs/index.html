This score represents the average of Sprint Readiness and Backlog Health across all five teams.

How is this calculated?
We take the average Sprint Readiness (stories in “To Do” or “Ready for Development”) and Backlog Health (stories in “New”, “Grooming”, “Backlog”) for each team. Then we average across all five teams.

Why it matters:
This summary gives a real-time pulse check on team delivery readiness and grooming discipline. It highlights whether the program is sprint-ready and operating with a healthy backlog.
