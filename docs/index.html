These dashboards are designed to inform—not punish.

Our goal is to provide objective visibility into the state of story ticket flow across the program. This summary aggregates the status of upcoming sprints and backlog grooming across all teams. Use this as a conversation starter and improvement guide.

Overall Program Health is derived from Sprint Readiness and Backlog Health calculations, following guidance from the Scrum Guide, Agile Manifesto, and State of Agile.

  • Sprint Readiness: % of story tickets in future sprints marked as “To Do” or “Ready for Development”
  • Backlog Health: % of all non-done stories in grooming statuses assigned to future sprints
  • Each team has separate calculations displayed on their dedicated tabs

Overall Program Status

This light reflects an aggregate score across all teams using weighted logic for Sprint Readiness and Backlog Health. See other dashboard pages for team-level breakdowns, exact metrics, and improvement opportunities.
