Program Summary

This page provides a snapshot of overall program health by combining Sprint Readiness and Backlog Health scores from all teams.

The stoplight indicator below reflects the average of these two metrics:

Green – Overall program health is on track.

Calculated using:

  • Sprint Readiness: % of story tickets in future sprints that are in 'To Do' or 'Ready for Development'
  • Backlog Health: % of story tickets in backlog statuses ('New', 'Grooming') assigned to future sprints

Scoring thresholds:

  • Green: ≥ 80%
  • Yellow: 50% – 79%
  • Red: < 50%
References: Scrum.org: Sprint Planning | SAFe PI Planning | Mountain Goat Software: Agile Stories

This dashboard follows Agile reporting best practices to ensure visibility, accountability, and readiness across teams.
