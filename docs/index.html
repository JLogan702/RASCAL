How This Was Calculated

Sprint Readiness reflects how many story tickets in upcoming sprints are in “To Do” or “Ready for Development.”

Backlog Health measures the percent of story tickets in future sprints or backlog that are groomed (“New”, “Grooming”, or “Backlog”).

Each team receives a score, and the program-level score is averaged across all five teams. Done, Blocked, and Cancelled stories are excluded.

Executive Interpretation Guidance

  • Green (≥ 80%): Program is well-prepared with clear, actionable work.
  • Yellow (50–79%): Sprint prep or backlog refinement may need attention.
  • Red (< 50%): Refinement action required — upcoming work is unclear or underprepared.
