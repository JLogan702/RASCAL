Program Summary

How Overall Program Health Is Calculated This dashboard blends Sprint Readiness and Backlog Health scores for each team, then averages those scores to determine overall health. Only story tickets assigned to future sprints or backlog are considered, excluding anything in “Done,” “In Progress,” “Blocked,” or “Cancelled.”

- Scrum.org defines Sprint Backlog as “the set of Product Backlog items selected for the Sprint…with a plan for delivering the product Increment and realizing the Sprint Goal.”
- Agile Alliance notes that backlog refinement “ensures stories are well understood and actionable before they are selected for sprint planning.”
- Martin Fowler states that a healthy backlog is “continuously updated and triaged to keep it ready for delivery.”

These dashboards are designed to help teams reflect and adjust — not to penalize. All data is real, traceable, and aligned to industry best practices.
