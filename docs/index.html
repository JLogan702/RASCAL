How This Was Calculated

Sprint Readiness measures how many story tickets in upcoming sprints are in a "ready" state (specifically "Ready for Development" or "To Do").

Backlog Health reflects the proportion of story tickets in future sprints or backlog that are groomed (statuses like "New", "Grooming", or "Backlog").

Each score is calculated per team, and the program-level score is the average across all five core teams. Done, Blocked, and Cancelled items are excluded.

Executive Interpretation Guidance

  • Green (≥ 80%): The program is well-prepared for upcoming sprints and has a healthy backlog.
  • Yellow (50–79%): The program may need attention to sprint prep or backlog grooming.
  • Red (< 50%): Immediate action is recommended to address gaps in sprint readiness or backlog quality.
