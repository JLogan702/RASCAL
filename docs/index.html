Program Summary

This page provides a snapshot of overall program health by combining Sprint Readiness and Backlog Health scores across all teams.

The stoplight indicator below reflects the average of these two metrics:

51.0% — Overall program health is on track based on readiness and backlog pipeline coverage.

Calculated using:

  • Sprint Readiness: % of story tickets in future sprints that are in 'To Do' or 'Ready for Development'
  • Backlog Health: % of story tickets in backlog statuses (‘New’, ‘Grooming’, ‘Backlog’, ‘Ready for Development’), excluding any completed or sprinted items

Scoring thresholds:

  • Green: ≥ 80%
  • Yellow: 50% – 79%
  • Red: < 50%

Note: This dashboard is a tool for teams — not a judgment. It highlights where support may be needed and fosters visibility, coordination, and better delivery forecasting.
