Program Summary

This page provides a snapshot of overall program health by combining Sprint Readiness and Backlog Health scores from all teams.

The stoplight indicator below reflects the average of these two metrics:

44.3% – Overall program health is calculated and color-coded accordingly.

How It’s Calculated

  • Sprint Readiness: % of story tickets in future sprints that are in 'To Do' or 'Ready for Development'
  • Backlog Health: % of story tickets in backlog statuses ('New', 'Grooming', 'Backlog', 'Ready for Development') assigned to non-active sprints

Scoring thresholds

  • Green: ≥ 80%
  • Yellow: 50% – 79%
  • Red: < 50%

Purpose

This dashboard is not meant to punish or evaluate individual performance. It exists to highlight where teams might need support, ensure readiness across the organization, and help teams spot trends early. This is a tool to empower teams to succeed—not to judge them.
