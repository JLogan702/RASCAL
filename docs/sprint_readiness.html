Sprint Readiness

Sprint Readiness is based on story tickets assigned to future sprints that are in 'To Do' or 'Ready for Development' status.
Statuses like 'In Progress', 'Done', or 'Blocked' are excluded. Stoplights reflect readiness % per team.

Source: Jira project filtered by component and sprint status. Based on Agile sprint planning best practices (Scrum.org).
