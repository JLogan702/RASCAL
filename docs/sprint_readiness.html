Sprint Readiness Dashboard

What is Sprint Readiness?
This metric shows how many story tickets are ready to be worked in the next sprint. Tickets must be:

  • Story-type
  • Assigned to a future sprint
  • In “To Do” or “Ready for Development” status

All statuses are shown per team for transparency, even if they don’t count toward readiness.
