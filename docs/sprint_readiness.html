Sprint Readiness Dashboard

What is Sprint Readiness?
This metric reflects how well teams are preparing their next sprint. We calculate it based on the number of story tickets assigned to a future sprint that are in either “Ready for Development” or “To Do” status.

Why it matters:
Teams that lack prepared work may enter sprints with uncertainty, increasing the risk of delays, idle time, or missed commitments.

How to read this:
The percentage indicates how many of a team's future-sprint story tickets are in a ready state. All statuses are shown for transparency, but only “To Do” and “Ready for Development” count toward readiness.
