Sprint Readiness Dashboard

What We Look At
- Included: Only story tickets in future sprints
- Counted as “Ready”: Tickets in Ready for Development or To Do status
- Not Counted: Tickets still in New, Grooming, or Backlog
- Excluded: Tickets not in a future sprint or not story type

Sprint Readiness Calculation
% Ready = (Stories in “To Do” or “Ready for Development”) ÷ (All story tickets in future sprints)

What the Percentages Mean
✅ Green: 80% or more are ready
🟡 Yellow: 50–79% are ready
🔴 Red: Less than 50% are ready

Industry References
- Scrum.org: 2–3 sprints planned ahead, not velocity
- Atlassian: Status flow Grooming → Ready
- SAFe: Sprint Planning Readiness
