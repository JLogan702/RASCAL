Program Status Overview

This dashboard provides an aggregated view of our program's health, covering Sprint Readiness, Backlog Health, and Epic Dependencies. Each metric follows industry-standard agile/Scrum methodologies to ensure accurate and transparent reporting.

The overall program status is determined by the combined health of these key areas. A single 'Red' area pulls the overall status to Red. Any 'Yellow' areas (without a 'Red' area) result in an overall Yellow status. Only if all areas are 'Green' will the program be 'Green'.

Backlog Health

**How Derived:** Percentage of story tickets in the active backlog (future sprints or unassigned) that are 'Ready' (in 'To Do' status). Higher % indicates a robust pipeline of defined work.
