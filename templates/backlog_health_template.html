Backlog Health

Note: This report is a diagnostic tool to guide team focus and backlog health — not a performance metric.

Definition: Backlog Health measures how many story tickets are in "New", "Grooming", or "Backlog" statuses and are not in active sprints. A high percentage means more groomed work is available for planning.

Industry Reference: See Scrum.org Product Backlog Guide, Section: “Refinement”; and Atlassian Backlog Best Practices.
