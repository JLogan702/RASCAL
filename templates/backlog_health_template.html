Backlog Health

Definition: Backlog Health is the percentage of story tickets in statuses “New”, “Grooming”, or “Backlog” that are not in active sprints. This helps teams maintain enough groomed work.

Industry Reference: See the Scrum.org Product Backlog Guide and Atlassian Backlog Best Practices.
