This score represents the average of Sprint Readiness and Backlog Health across all five teams.

What This Represents:
This score reflects overall program readiness in terms of sprint planning discipline and backlog grooming health.

How is this calculated?
We average each team’s Sprint Readiness (% of stories in To Do or Ready for Development in future sprints) and Backlog Health (% of stories in Ready or To Do status across future sprint and backlog). Then we take the mean across all teams.

Why it matters:
This gives leadership a real-time pulse check on delivery readiness, grooming habits, and overall Agile health of the program.
