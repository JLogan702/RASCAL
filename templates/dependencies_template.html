Dependency Tracker

Why this matters

Dependencies can create serious bottlenecks in Agile delivery. Identifying blocked or blocking stories helps proactively mitigate delays across teams.

This tracker identifies Jira story tickets with blocking relationships ("Blocks" links) either inward or outward. These relationships reflect tickets that must be completed before others can start or finish.

Source: Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe), Atlassian Dependency Management Practices

References:
