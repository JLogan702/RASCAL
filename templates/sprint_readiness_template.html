Sprint Readiness

Note: This report is intended to support team planning and predictability — not to judge performance.

Definition: Sprint Readiness is the percentage of story tickets in future sprints that are in "To Do" or "Ready for Development" statuses.

Industry Reference: See Scrum.org Sprint Planning Guide, Section: “Forecasting Sprint Backlog”; and Atlassian Sprint Planning Best Practices.
